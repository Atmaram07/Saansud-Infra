SAANSUD Laxmi Narayan Vihar

Gated Community in Paradeep

Project Overview

Imagine owning your dream home in a prime Paradeep ​neighborhood and enjoying a 3X return on investment in a short ​period, surrounded by stunning views that create a peaceful ​atmosphere.

SAANSUD Laxmi Narayan Vihar, a flagship project, presents 59 ​meticulously planned plots spread over 1.30 Lakh square feet. ​Located on the Paradeep-Kujang Road, this gated community offers ​approved residential plots. This area is increasingly favored by ​homebuyers due to rising commercial activities, enhanced amenities, ​and improved infrastructure.

The project provides recreational amenities like a Community Hall, ​Kids' Gaming Area, Security, and a Temple, among others. These ​premium plots are conveniently located near top schools, hospitals, ​banks, shopping centers, and hotels. SAANSUD Laxmi Narayan ​Vihar boasts quality infrastructure with concrete roads, tree-lined ​avenues, boundary walls, street lighting, walkways, and stormwater ​drainage.

Invest in SAANSUD Laxmi Narayan Vihar today and fulfill your ​dream of building a home in this exceptional location!
